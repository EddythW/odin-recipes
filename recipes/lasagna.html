Easy Lasagna

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesnâ€™t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

  • Beef
  • Lasagna Sheet
  • Morzella Cheese
  • Boiling Water
  • Spice of Choice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, adding spices and slate
  2. Boil Water and place lasagna sheets one by one soaking them in water
  3. Remove sheets in water to assemble lasagna
  4. Place some sheets on a baking pan followed by ground beef then cheese
  5. Repeat step four and top the lasagna with cheese
  6. Into the oven and bake covered for 45 minutes
  7. Uncover bake for 5 to 10 mins and remove from oven
